Transform the Way You Read and Explore Literature!

Our app was designed to help students overcome common challenges in reading literary works. While these works are rich in meaning, they can often feel intimidating. The app organizes information in a simple way, making reading easier.

 

How does RELIA work?

Created to democratize and enhance the experience of attentive reading, RELIA follows a three-step framework:

  1. Contextualization: Explore the initial aspects of the work, including its historical context and main themes.
  2. Analysis and Interpretation: Dive deeper into critical reflection and build conceptual connections.
  3. Expand and connect: Relate the ideas from the work to other books, contexts, and themes that spark your interest.
